About

For over two decades, author and speaker Rev. Dave Thompson has continued his work building peace and common ground among our most divided social groups, organizations and politics.

Rev Dave's long career of bringing people together has helped him develop a real-world method for building common ground. His CIVIL HUMAN™ Ethic provides a standard of best-practices and rules for avoiding many of the road blocks that can derail the process. Placing himself at the epicenter of some of today's most divisive issues, Rev. Dave has become an invaluable resource for leaders hoping to better understand the issues, the people and the culture surrounding our divisions.

Rev. Dave’s disarming and engaging personality continues to leave his audience with expanded horizons and a renewed hope for peace.

  • 1995

    Ministry Education

    Northwest University

    Rev. Dave began his career as a Christian Minister, studying with a BA in Religion & Philosophy, while also focusing on ancient culture and the translation of ancient Biblical texts.

  • 1998

    Theology Education

    Gordon-Conwell Theological Seminary

    Rev. Dave continued his ministry studies, entering a Masters in Theology program, forcusing on conflict resolution. Additionally, he worked to receive a Certificate in Religion & Social Conflict from the Boston Theological Institute, while also working in distributed and online education.

  • 2000

    Education Administration

    Northwest University

    Being disillusioned with his lack of experience in the business and politics of social conflict, Rev. Dave continued his work in Education Administration, focusing on non-traditional students and serving as the Director of Online Education. He spent these years reflecting on his next course of action.

  • 2003

    Political Consulting

    Policorp Corporation

    In an effort to broaden his understand of social conflict, Rev. Dave left formal ministry and launched a political consulting firm. During these years he was fortunate to work with local, state, national and international political campaigns, managing communications and public relations. Finding he had a knack for software, he also built one of the first open source software platforms to manage political campaigns.

  • 2007

    Fortune 500 & Tech

    Starbucks Corporation

    While closing his political consulting firm, Rev. Dave worked as a Barista at Starbucks. He was then promoted, becoming the Lead Application Developer for Starbucks' Global Business Services Solutions.

  • 2009

    Recorded Musician

    Disclaimer CD

    Rev. Dave has spent most of his life performing music (piano/singing) in venues of all sorts. In 2009 he decided to check a box off his bucket list, and put out a cd of his own original compositions called Disclaimer.

  • 2010

    Author & Speaker

    Over Coffee

    After years of reseach and reflection, Rev. Dave returned to formal ministry, launching his first book, Over Coffee: a conversation for gay partnership and conservative faith. This was his chance to work on a piece of religion and social conflict that was close to home. Since he began, he has reached across the political and spiritual divides to meet privately, over coffee, with hundreds of conservative and liberal ministers and leaders, providing a safe-space for them to talk about gay (and other sexuality) issues in their ministries, organizations or personal lives.

  • 2019

    YouTube Personality

    CIVIL HUMAN Bible Study

    Rev. Dave launched a YouTube weekdays@noon study searching for human common ground from within his own spiritual heritage. His unique approach led him to consider the Gospel of Matthew, from a purely HUMAN perspective, attempting to find those teachings that every person can hold in common, regardless of their race, creed or color.

  • Coming Soon!

    Second Book

    Becoming HUMAN

    In this gripping near-death survival story, Rev Dave shares his harrowing account of being trapped on a mountaintop, in the grip of a winter storm.

    In the midst of each new challenge, we get a unique look into the spiritual and philisophic struggles of a Christian Minister as he stands on the precipace of death. With each new development we begin to see Rev Dave pulling back layer after layer of his beliefs, until he is left to wrestle with only those truths that we as human beings must acknowledge.
