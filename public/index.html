We are now a world
without clear borders.

For the first time in our human history, we are no longer separated by the great distances of land and sea. In today's world, people from every nation interact with each other on a daily basis.

We are now a world
without a common culture.

For the first time as humans we are facing a world where we are challenged to manage the influence of every culture in our world at the same time.

Even still...

We hold this in common;
Together we are human.

Underneath all of our many differences and divisions, we share the same basic human needs. It's in this space where we can find the common ground needed to find lasting peace.
