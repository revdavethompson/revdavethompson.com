A CIVIL HUMAN Ethic

For over twenty years, Rev. Dave has worked to build common ground and peace within our most divided of organizations, faith groups, and politics. His long work and experience gave him the opportunity to discover best-practices that moved people more quickly to common ground, and helped to maintain that peace.

As a CIVIL HUMAN Servant, Rev. Dave now shares his CIVIL HUMAN Ethic; teaching people, businessess and organizations the best practices for overcoming division, building common gournd, and establishing a peaceful culture.

H.U.M.A.N. Ethics

These ethics represent some of the most fundamental qualities by which every person must measure themselves if we hope to build the common ground necessary for a culture of peace.

  • H

    Honest

    We must be honest about what we observe and what we cannot observe.

  • U

    Understandable

    We must work to build understanding, so that every person can truly understand what is being agreed upon.

  • M

    Measurable

    We must provide measurable proof for the claims that we make.

  • A

    Achievable

    We must only require of others what is achievable without suffering.

  • N

    Negotiable

    In an ever-changing universe, we must agree to be negotiable with our rules, so they continue to remain CIVIL and HUMAN.

C.I.V.I.L. Rights

These rights represent our most fundamental and divisive of human needs. Each person must be free to disagree on these matters if we wish to maintain our common ground and peace.

  • C

    Creed

    We must respect every person's right to form their own conclusions (faith) about what we cannot observe.

  • I

    Independence

    We must respect every person's right to be independent from the control of others.

  • V

    Vote

    We must respect every person's right to choose those who will have control over them.

  • I

    Intimacy

    We must respect every person's right to intimately love the person of their own choosing.

  • L

    Lifestyle

    We must respect every person's right to live, and to pursue a lifestyle that makes them happy.
