A HUMAN Ethic

In the age of the Internet, our once clear notions of culture are disappearing. Will we continue to fight over our cultures, or can we identify the ethics we share, that can help us find the common ground necessary to create a lasting peace?

Rev. Dave Thompson has spent over twenty years building common ground in our most divided of social groups and issues. His long experience provided him the opportunity to develop his HUMAN Ethic, a set of real-world, field-tested rules of conduct necessary for finding the common ground that creates peace.

H.U.M.A.N. Ethics

These five standards represent the most basic and necessary criteria for creating the rules that all people can hold in common.

  • H

    Honest

    We must be honest about what we can and cannot observe or measure.

  • U

    Understandable

    We must work to build understanding, so that every person can truly understand what is being agreed upon.

  • M

    Measurable

    We must provide measurable proof for the claims that we make and accept.

  • A

    Achievable

    We must only require of others what is achievable without suffering.

  • N

    Negotiable

    In an ever-changing universe, we must agree to be negotiable with our rules, so they continue to remain CIVIL and HUMAN.
